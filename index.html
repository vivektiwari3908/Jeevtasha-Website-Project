We Work For Humanity

Jeevtasha Foundation is a non-profit dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and sustainable development initiatives.

About Us

Founded by passionate changemakers, Jeevtasha Foundation envisions a compassionate society where every individual lives with dignity and opportunity. Our mission is to empower communities through impactful initiatives in education, environment, food security, and healthcare.

Our Programs

Cloth Donation Drive

Providing clothes to underprivileged communities for dignity and comfort.

Plantation Drive

Promoting environmental sustainability through tree plantation initiatives.

Education Drive

Distributing books, stationery, and providing education support to children.

Food Donation Drive

Serving nutritious meals to fight hunger and ensure food security.
